Three trips that stuck with me

Ranked, with a few notes on each.

  1. 01

    Sydney, Australia

    Sep 2023 – Feb 2025

    • Bondi to Coogee walk is still my favorite thing I've ever done on a coastline
  2. 02

    Hong Kong, China

    Jun 2025 – Aug 2025
    • Led a group of youth on a 5-week humanitarian trip with HXP

  3. 03

    Maastricht, Netherlands

    Jul 2021 – Aug 2021

    • Easy train rides to Paris, Brussels, and Amsterdam on the weekends
